About Us

Welcome to Bruce Bay Beans and Bunks, a small family-owned business located on the picturesque Haast Highway between Haast and Fox Glacier. Formerly known as the Bruce Bay Coffee Cart, we have evolved from a mobile coffee cart into a permanent building offering both delicious food and drinks as well as cozy lodging for tired tourists.

Our Story

Our journey began with a simple coffee cart, serving travelers with freshly brewed coffee and tasty snacks. As our popularity grew, so did our aspirations. We decided to establish a permanent location to better serve our customers. Today, Bruce Bay Beans and Bunks is not only a spot to grab a quick bite but also a place to rest and rejuvenate.

What We Offer

We offer a wide range of food and drink options, including:

  • Freshly brewed coffee with special milks and syrups
  • Soft drinks, milk shakes, and ice teas
  • Snacks like American hotdogs, sweetcorn & kumara patties, crepes with various toppings, Smokey BBQ pulled pork in a bun, muffins, slices, ice blocks, and real fruit ice creams
  • Essential items such as insect spray and Bruce Bay souvenir merchandise

Our Accommodation

We offer comfortable lodging options, including:

  • A dorm room with four single bunks, which can be booked individually
  • A twin room with two single beds (capacity: two people)
  • A room with a queen bed and pull-out sofa (capacity: three people)

All guests have access to shared bathroom and kitchen facilities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a warm and welcoming environment for all our guests. Whether you're stopping by for a quick coffee or staying overnight, we aim to make your experience as pleasant and comfortable as possible.
